Duckbreast Recipe

Seared duckbreast with garlic mash potatoe and green beans

Succulent duckbreast seasoned with chinese 5-spice, accompanied by garlic mash potato and roasted green beans.

Ingredients

  • Duckbreast x2
  • Green beans 500g
  • Large potatoes x2
  • Chinese 5-spice seasoning
  • Butter 200g
  • Rosemary 50g

Steps

  1. Boil the large potatoes until soft (approx 20 minutes)
  2. Score the fat of the duck breast in a criss-cross pattern
  3. Season the duckbreast well with five spice
  4. Sear the duck breast on a pan, fat side first, without pre heating the pan. Cook until the skin is crisp.
  5. Turn the duckbreast over and base with garlic, butter, and rosemary.
  6. Pre-heat the oven to 180 C, then lightly oil the green beans and season with salt and pepper. Cook for approximaely 12 minutes.
  7. Drain the potatoes, mash them in the same pan on a low heat while adding garlic, butter, and milk until creamy in texture.
  8. Plate up the duckbreast, green beans, and mash potatoe side by side.
  9. Pour the duck fat over the garlic mash potatoe
