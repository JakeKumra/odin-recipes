Garlic Chicken Recipe

Creamy, garlic lemon chicken

A dish that explodes with flavor and richness that contains a creamy garlic lemon sauce with seared chicken breasts on a bed of roasted potatoes and a side of asparagus.

Ingredients

  • Shallots
  • Head of garlic x1
  • White wine 250ml
  • Chicken stock cube
  • Single cream 100ml
  • Parsley
  • Chicken breasts x2
  • Medium sized potatoes x3
  • Asparagus 400g
  • Butter 100g
  • Mushrooms 250g
  • Lemon 1/2

Steps

  1. Cut the head off the top of the garlic head and roast for an hour at 180 C
  2. Dice the potatoes and roast them for 45 minutes on a baking pan with salt and pepper and oregano seasoning
  3. Season the asparagus and add sear on medium-high heat 20 minutes before serving.
  4. Cut the chicken breast in half, and sear until lighty brown, then remove from pan (don't clean the pan and remember to season as you add new ingredients
  5. Finely dice shallots and add to the same pan with olive oil and cook until lightly browned
  6. Add 50g butter and stir
  7. Add 50ml of white wine
  8. Add mushrooms and cook on medium heat
  9. Take the garlic from the oven and squeeze heads into a bowl and mash together, then add this to the pan
  10. Add the cream and chicken stock cube and stir contents
  11. Add the chicken back to the pan and stir until ready to be served.
  12. Add a pinch of fresh parsley, and squeeze lemon over the pan
  13. Pour the contents onto the bed of potatoes on a plate and put the asparagus on the side.
  14. Season as required
